Code of Conduct

The Club's Spirit — the standards we expect from every member, centred on camaraderie, respect and responsible riding.

♡

Community & Welcoming

  • Ride together wearing club jerseys whenever possible.
  • Greet fellow cyclists politely and in friendly fashion.
  • Make first-time riders and visitors feel particularly welcome.
  • Ride captains should welcome newcomers at ride starts.
⚖

Respect & Conduct

"Club members are expected at all times to treat other club members, other cyclists, motorists and other road users with the utmost respect."

  • Unlawful conduct or actions bringing disrepute to the club are prohibited.
  • Courteous cycling is encouraged.
  • No verbal altercations or obscene gestures toward road users.
⛑

Safety Responsibilities

  • Ride responsibly without jeopardising cyclist safety.
  • "We never leave club members with a puncture or mechanical fault alone" during official rides.
  • Follow ride captains' and marshals' instructions.
  • Stop at red lights, junctions and intersections.
⚐

Environmental & Professional Standards

  • Do not litter — no energy sachets, tubes or wrappers left behind.
  • Wear sponsor colours and club jerseys at races.
  • Minimum 6 rides yearly and one club function required for membership in good standing.
Consequences

The line we don't cross.

"Failure to do so may result in disciplinary action and expulsion from the club."
