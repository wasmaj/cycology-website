Board of Trustees

The trustees who provide governance, oversight and long-term direction for Cycology Cycling Club.

Our Board of Trustees is composed of senior members who safeguard the club's mission, values and not-for-profit status. They work alongside the Club Executives to ensure Cycology remains a force for good in cycling and the wider community.
