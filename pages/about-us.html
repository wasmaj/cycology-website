About Cycology

A friendly cycling club in Lagos — with a serious mission, a strong community, and a fifteen-year history of moving people forward on two wheels.

Who we are

Friendly. Professional. Passionate about cycling.

Cycology is a friendly cycling club in Lagos, with over 300 members passionate about cycling. We were established in 2011 as a not-for-profit organisation and have been growing steadily ever since.

Our membership is comprised of professionals and successful entrepreneurs from the public and private sectors, interested in networking with people of like mind. The club provides weekly scheduled rides, and we use a virtual clubhouse on Telegram and WhatsApp as a meeting space so the community keeps moving every day of the week.

Road cycling is our primary activity, though some members engage in fat bikes, mountain bikes, and hybrid off-road cycling. We share ride data via Strava in our members-only group and sponsor friendly segment competitions throughout the year.

Our Mission

  • Partner with government entities for road safety education

  • Build partnerships benefiting cyclists and society
