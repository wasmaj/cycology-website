About Cycology

A friendly cycling club in Lagos — with a serious mission, a strong community, and a fifteen-year history of moving people forward on two wheels.

Who we are

Friendly. Professional. Passionate about cycling.

Cycology is a friendly cycling club in Lagos with over 300 members passionate about cycling. We offer weekly scheduled club rides and non-club rides, fostering a vibrant community for cycling enthusiasts.

Our virtual clubhouse on the Telegram forum helps us organise rides and engage socially. While road cycling remains our primary focus, many members also explore fat bike, mountain bike and hybrid off-road cycling.

We share our rides on our Strava club group, where there's some healthy (but sensible) competition for certain segments. The Strava group is for members only.

Our Vision

To create a global platform for promoting cycling as a tool for a healthy lifestyle and social development.

Our Mission

  • Promote cycling as a fun and healthy lifestyle
  • Collaborate with Government Agencies to enlighten road users
  • Draw attention to social issues through cycling
  • Promote grassroots and professional cycling
  • Forge collaborative partnerships that positively impact the cycling community and our society
