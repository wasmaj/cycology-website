Corporate Social Responsibility

A not-for-profit cycling club established in 2011 with a vision to create a global platform for promoting cycling as a tool for a healthy lifestyle and social development.

Our Partners

Timeline

A decade of riding for good.

A snapshot of our CSR activities between 2014 and 2020.

2014–2015

Cancer Awareness Rides

Multiple rides supporting breast cancer awareness and general cancer education across Lagos.

2016

500 Books to a Skills Centre

A book donation drive supplying a Lagos skills centre with educational material.

2017

World Autism Awareness

Campaign rides in support of World Autism Awareness Day and partner organisations.

2018

Share the Road launches

Cycology launches the Share the Road (STR) campaign for traffic safety education and behaviour change.

2018

Polio Eradication Fundraising

Joint fundraising with Rotary Club towards global polio eradication.

2019–2020

Food for Street Sweepers

Food assistance distributed to Lagos street sweepers across multiple ride drops.

2020

COVID-19 Relief

Delivery of relief supplies to COVID-19 isolation centres in Lagos at the height of the pandemic.

Ongoing

Bicycles for Rural Students

Bicycle and helmet donations to rural school children through our partnership with the African Cycling Foundation.
