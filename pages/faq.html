Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first ride with Cycology.

How do I join the club?

Cycology is open to any professional with an interest in cycling. Complete the membership form, then pay the signup and annual subscription fees if approved.

Start your application →

At what pace are the rides?

Speed bands range from Group A (35+ kph) down to Group E (15-20 kph steady pace). New riders who are unsure of their capabilities are typically advised to join either Group D or E. Sprints can exceed 40 kph.

Can I participate without being a member?

Club events are for members only, except for one-off campaigns and races open to the public. Non-members are also welcome at the Coffee Ride, which we hold a minimum of twice a year.

What time does the club ride?

Official Cycology rides take place every Saturday from either 6:00am (hot season) or 6:15am (cool season) prompt.

Who do I contact with other questions?

Fill out the Contact Us form on the contact page and we will get back to you.

Contact us →

How much are club dues?

Members are notified of dues when applying for membership.

How many km are the rides?

The Saturday club ride is typically 70km and above for groups A-C, about 50km for group D, and 35km for group E.

What is a Coffee Ride?

An initiative of the club to encourage non-members to participate in cycling — usually at a gentle pace over a short distance, terminating at a coffee place for socialising. A great way to try the Cycology vibe before applying for membership.

Is there a minimum ride participation requirement?

Yes — each member is required to participate in a minimum of 6 rides a year.

Can I get a jersey without membership?

No. You must be a member or affiliate to purchase a jersey.

Where can I get a bicycle?

Cycology doesn't rent or sell bicycles. Three retailers we recommend:

  • Vantage Sport
  • FT Cycle Care
  • Xbikes
