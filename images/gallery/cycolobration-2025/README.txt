CYCOLOBRATION 2025 ALBUM
========================

Drop your photos into this folder. They appear automatically on the
gallery page at /pages/gallery.html.

Supported formats: .jpg .jpeg .png .webp .gif .avif

To control the display order, name your files numerically:
  photo-001.jpg
  photo-002.jpg
  photo-003.jpg

Hidden files (anything starting with a dot) and this README are
ignored by the gallery scanner.
