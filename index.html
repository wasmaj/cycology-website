About Cycology

A friendly club. A serious mission.

Cycology is a friendly cycling club in Lagos with over 300 members passionate about cycling. We offer weekly scheduled club rides and non-club rides, fostering a vibrant community for cycling enthusiasts.

Our virtual clubhouse on the Telegram forum keeps the community moving every day of the week. Road cycling is our primary focus, though many members also enjoy fat bike, mountain bike and hybrid off-road cycling.

What we stand for

Our mission, in five turns of the pedal.

Cycology exists to promote cycling as enjoyable, beneficial to wellness, and a meaningful tool for road safety advocacy and community development.

How we ride

Rides for every level.

From sunrise hammerfests to gentle Coffee Rides, there's a wheel for everyone. Pick the group that matches your fitness on the day.

Saturday · 6:00am / 6:15am

Saturday Club Ride

The main event. Departs from the Ikoyi clubhouse — 6:00am in the hot season, 6:15am in the cool season — with a ride captain appointed daily. Typically 70km+ for groups A–C, 50km for D and 35km for E.
