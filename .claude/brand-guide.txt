   INTRODUCTION

   PLEASE NOTE:
   No changes or own creations are allowed.
   All branding processes are managed and controlled by Cycology Riding Club.

   APPLICATION:
   The Corporate Identity Design Guideline applies to all measures with which Cycology present itself to the
   external world.

   1. Print
   Advertisements, Mails, Reports, Letters, Flyers, Posters, etc.

   2. Online
   Websites, Social Media, etc.

   3. Other
   Riding Uniforms, Vehicle Livery, Collateral, Sponsorship Agreements, etc.

| CORPORATE IDENTITY GENERAL GUIDELINES / VERSION 2 / AUGUST 2018
   LOGO

   The logo is a collective of shapes that, when put together, form a stylised
   bicycle, the activity that unites the organisation. The bicycle is an instantly
   recognisable icon. It represents the club membership and also enables
   activity recognition by those who are not aware or affiliated with the club.

   The various shapes and how these are made up represent the network and
   diversity of the club. It also references the role each club member plays in
   the organisation.

   The identity's use of colour has expanded and underpins the representation
   of diversity in the club. Pink has been aded as an identity colour to
   represent the increase in female club members while also representing the
   club's approach to inclusivity and belonging.

   The colours have been adjusted to be within the same tonal value,
   representing the singular vision and value system of the club while at the
   same time celebrating diversity. The colours are bolder to portray more
   youthfulness, fun, energy and vibrancy.

   Letter type for the wordmark was selected based on its round wheel like
   qualities.

   The overall logo concept was kept abstract to represent all types of cycling
   rather than advancing road cycling over others.

   The logo is the most important visual recognition feature of a brand. It
   consists of the brand name `Cycology' (logotype) and the stylised icon.

   It is not allowed to create variations or change the logotype in any way.

                                                                                                                    1X                                                                                                                   1X
                                                                                                                    1X
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         1X
   X height is the equivalent to the height of the capital letter O.
   The logotype represents 1X while the icon is 3,6X in proportion to the logotype.                                     The exclusion zone is always no less than 1X all round.
   The logotype is centred with the icon.                                                                               The exclusion zone represents the dedicated zone for use of the logo. This zone
                                                                                                                        must remain blank to allow printing the logo on a coloured background which would
                                                                                                                        otherwise prevent the reader from identifying the logo. If the background does not
                                                                                                                        allow the reader to identify the logo, the exclusion zone should include a
                                                                                                                        white background.
                                                                                                                        The exclusion zone also prohibits any other elements such as typography,
                                                                                                                        pattern or shapes to introduce within this zone.
